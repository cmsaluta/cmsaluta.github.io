About

I'm a full stack web developer specializing in Python (Django), with strong experience in React.js, React Native, and iOS development. I have a solid understanding of AI and machine learning, and I work confidently with databases like MySQL, MSSQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. I also have DevOps experience, particularly in setting up and managing applications on virtual machines to ensure scalable and efficient deployment environments.

Beyond development, I take on leadership roles in guiding teams and coordinating cross-functional collaboration. I'm experienced in preparing technical documentation and generating analytical reports for both internal use and external stakeholders—supporting data-driven decision-making and project transparency.
